About

This is a photo collection of textural surfaces. The point is to get in tune with the minute details of our world. As life becomes increasingly reliant on technology, it is easy to move too quickly to notice or enjoy the variety of patterns, textures, and physical surfaces we interact with. This collection aims to bring attention to physicality and materiality that exists beyond the screen with close-up images of many different objects for the viewer to enjoy. Hopefully it will encourage the viewer to be impressed by the beauty of small stuff that is everywhere around us, and make a greater effort to notice it in their daily lives.
